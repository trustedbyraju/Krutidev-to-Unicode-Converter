About This Tool

The Krutidev to Unicode Converter was built to solve a widespread problem faced by millions of Hindi content creators, journalists, government employees, teachers, and students across India who work with legacy Krutidev-encoded documents.

Krutidev was one of India's most popular Hindi typing fonts in the 1990s–2000s. While it served an important purpose, its text cannot be read, searched, or used in modern digital systems without the font installed. This tool bridges that gap by providing accurate, instant conversion to Unicode — the modern universal standard.

The converter uses a comprehensive character mapping table built from years of Devanagari linguistics research, ensuring near-perfect accuracy for all standard Krutidev text including conjunct characters, matras, and special glyphs.
